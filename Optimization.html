Control Layouts

My favorite layouts and tips to improve gameplay comfort.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

CD Projekt Red Official Layout (with enabled and remapped back button)

The official layout is solid, but I recommend trying to use the back buttons for longer handheld sessions. I mapped autosave to R4 and the left menu to R5, which reduces hand strain during handheld play.

On TV with an Xbox controller, the default layout works best for me

Stardew Valley

Custom Layout: Uses the Steam Official Layout (Template) with added functions of community-made layouts

The official layout works perfectly, but I also enjoy experimenting with community layouts. My favorite adds touchpad functionality, which I’ve integrated into my custom layout.

Controller support is extensive, and mixing official and community templates gives the best of both worlds.

My setup:

    Official - Steam - (Traackpads)
    Stardew Modified Xbox - Teruen - (ABXY inputs, DPad Inputs)
    Steam Controller - Azazrael - (Triggers, R2, R1, L1, L2)

Persona 3: Reload

ATLUS: Persona 3: Reload Official Layout, with Gamepad (Back buttons disabled, remapped sprint button)

Hatsune Miku: MegaMix+

Custom Control Layout (uses SEGA official layout as template), Back buttons enabled, Trackpads disabled

I use the Future Tone settings instead of the default Megamix ones for all songs, since they feel more familiar.

My Back buttons are mapped to mirror the main ABXY inputs, making rhythm gameplay smoother.

Gyro and trackpad customization help with menu navigation, though I disable trackpads during songs, because it ruins combos. (I use the Xbox notes)

Note: Mix Mode is intended only for use on the Nintendo Switch!!

It's HIGHLY recommended that SteamDeck users (and PC users) play using Arcade Mode!
