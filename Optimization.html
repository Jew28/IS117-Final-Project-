Control Layouts

My favorite layouts and tips to improve gameplay comfort.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

Stardew Valley

Uses Steam Official Layout with added community features.

  • Trackpads: Steam Official
  • ABXY/DPad: Stardew Modified Xbox (Teruen)
  • Triggers: Steam Controller (Azazrael)

Mixing official and community templates gives the best of both worlds.

Persona 3: Reload

ATLUS Official Layout with Gamepad. Back buttons disabled, sprint remapped.

Hatsune Miku: MegaMix+

Custom layout based on SEGA official. Back buttons mirror ABXY inputs.

Trackpads disabled during songs to avoid combo breaks.

Gyro helps with menu navigation. Xbox note style preferred.

Note: Mix Mode is for Nintendo Switch only. SteamDeck users should use Arcade Mode.
