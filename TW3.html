The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

Developer: CD PROJEKT RED

Release Date: October 19 2015

Steam Deck Rating: Verified (one of the top played games on Steam Deck)

Gameplay balances exploration, monster hunting, and choice‑driven quests. The exploration for this game is so good, most times I enjoy walking around the map, looking for more side quests, rather than completing the main ones The Combat is so fluid, and it's very obvious that this was used as a stepping stone for Cyberpunk 2077's eventual combat system. Running the new‑gen version at 45 FPS makes long sessions manageable and allows for longer play sessions without a charger. (Personally, I prefer playing the game on my TV, with my SteamDeck connected via HDMI, with an Xbox controller The game reacts to all of your decisions, so dialogue is just as important as battles. Even the progression system feels intertwined with exploration, with you needing to kill different monsters for materials
