Persona 3: Reload

Developer: ATLUS / P-Studio

Release Date: February 2nd 2024

Steam Deck Rating: Verified

Persona 3: Reload is a remake of the original Persona 3 game from 2006. The remake modernises its gameplay to a more Persona 5 style, while retaining the charm of the original. The game is a mix of dungeon crawling in Tartarus and story‑driven character interactions and story battles during full moons. (and don't worry, you can still battle in Tartarus, as long as the main character still has stamina). I would know, because a majority of my 130 playthroughs have been spent in Tartarus, levelling up my team. To me, I prefer the structure of Persona 3 Reload to Persona 5, but both games have their own strengths and weaknesses, despite their similarities
